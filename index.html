Aiding Banoth's Mother with her medical expenses

Projects-29 Goal : 31,000 Amount-Raised : 0   Share

 WhatsApp  Facebook  Email

Banoth Kumar is in his second year of engineering at NIT Calicut, pursuing his education with the aid of student loan as his family is financially poor. He lost his father when he was young.His mother Lakshmi is suffering from throat infection and knee cap pain. They have consulted the doctor who has adviced to start treatment for her damaged knee cap.

Banoth has been working in the farms to gather money for treatment, but was able to save only 6k till now. As his mother's health is deteriorating, they are in dire need of the remaining amount at the earliest.Banoth approached Dreams Alive Foundation (DAF) in this critical time. We have verified the authenticity of the situation and helped Banoth with an amount of Rs 16500 for his mother’s treatment and also for purchasing necessary kneecap kit.
